DNCocktails

Drinks mit Irish Whiskey.

IRISH

Best to use Single Pot Still Irish Whiskey. Normal ones are blended with neutral grain alcohol.

BRAINSTORM

6cl Irish Whiskey

1cl Dry Vermouth

1cl Dom Benediktin

1,5cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Orange peel garnish.

CAMERON`S KICK

3cl Irish Whiskey

3cl Scotch

1,5cl Oregeat

1,5cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Shake. No ice double strain.

IRISH BLACKTHORN

6cl Irish Whiskey

3cl White Vermouth

2 Dash bitters

Half to 3/4 of egg white

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Cover glass with Absinth. Lemon peel garnish.

IRISH COFFEE

6cl Irish Whiskey

12cl Coffee

1 sugar cube

Zubereitung: Pour over whiped cream.

A James Bond favorite!

IRISH OLD FASHIONED

6cl Irish Whiskey

2,2cl Dom Benediktin

2 Dash orange bitters

2 Dash bitters

Zubereitung: Stir. Fill in OF glass with one big chunk of ice. Orange peel garnish.

TIPPERARY

6cl Irish Whiskey

3cl Red Vermouth

1,5cl Green Chartreuse

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Orange peel garnish.
