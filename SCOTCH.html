DNCocktails

Drinks mit Scotch. Scotch kommt nur aus Schottland und ist aus Gerste.

SCOTCH

BLACK WATCH

4,5cl Peated Scotch

2,2cl Coffee liqor

Zubereitung: Add all in OF glass with big ice pieces stir a little. Fill with Soda. Lemon peel garnish

BLOOD AND SAND

3cl Scotch

3cl Orange juice

2,2cl Cherry liqor

2,2cl Red Vermouth

Zubereitung: Shake. No ice, NO DOUBLE STRAIN. Orange peel garnish.

BOBBY BURNS

6cl Scotch

2,2cl Red Vermouth

1,5cl Dom Benediktin

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Lemon peel garnish.

FULL WINDSOR

3cl Scotch

3cl Apple Brandy

2,2cl Red Vermouth

0,8cl Dom Benediktin

2 Dash bitter

2 Dash Peychauds bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. Fill in OF glass with one big chunk of ice. Orange peel garnish.

Mamie Taylor

6cl Scotch

2,2cl Lime juice

Zubereitung: Add all in collins glass with big ice pieces. Fill with gingerbeer and garnish with lime wedge.

PENICILLIN

6cl Scotch

1,1cl Honey syrup

1,1cl Ginger syrup

2,2cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Shake. Double strain, fill in OF glass with one big chunk of ice. Top with 3xspray Islay Scotch. NO LEMON PEEL use candy ginger.

ROB ROY

6cl Scotch

2,2cl Red Vermouth

2 Dash orange bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. 3x Maraschino cherry on toothpick garnish.

SCOTCH LODGE

4,5cl Scotch

1,5cl Italian herb bitter (Ramazzotti, Cynar or others)

1,5cl Cherry Heering

1,5cl Red Vermouth

1 Dash orange bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. Fill in OF glass with one big chunk of ice. Orange peel garnish.

SCOTCH AND SODA

6cl Scotch

Zubereitung: Add Scotch in collins glass with big ice pieces. Fill with Soda.

A James Bond favorite!

SMOKY GRAIL

6cl Peated Scotch

2,2cl Singani

0,8cl Coffee liqor

1,5cl Dom Benediktin

Zubereitung: Stir. Fill in OF glass with one big chunk of ice. Orange peel garnish.

SMOKY GROVE

6cl Peated Scotch

1,5cl Red Vermouth

1,5cl Dry Vermouth

1 Dash orange bitter

1 Dash bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Flamed orange peel garnish.

TO HELL WITH SPAIN

6cl Scotch

1,5cl Red Vermouth

1,5cl Cherry liqor

0,8cl Peated Scotch

3 Dash Absinth

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Maraschino cherry garnish.

UNHOLY ALLIANCE

4,5cl Scotch

3cl Red Vermouth

3cl Campari

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Orange peel garnish.

Woolworth

6cl Scotch

1,5cl Dry Sherry

1,5cl Dom Benediktin

2 Dash orange bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Lemon peel garnish.
