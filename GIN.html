Drinks mit Gin.

GIN

AVIATION

6cl Gin

1,5cl Marascino Liqor

0,75cl Creme de violette

2,2cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Shake. Double strain no ice. Marascino cherry garnish.

BEE´S KNEES

4,5cl Gin

2,2cl Honey syrup(3:1)

2,2cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Shake. Double strain no ice. Orange peel garnish.

DRY MARTINI

6cl Gin

1,5cl Dry Vermouth

1 Dash orange bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. No Ice double strain. Lemon peel garnish.

A James Bond favorite!

GIMLET

6cl Gin

3cl Lime Corddial(1:1 If like sweeter)

Zubereitung: Stir. No Ice. Lime peel garnish.

GIN TONIC 007

6cl Gin

1 Lime juice direct into glass

Zubereitung: Build in GT glass with ice. Top with ca 18cl Tonic. Squeezed Lime half garnish.

A James Bond favorite!

PENGU CLUB

4,5cl Gin

2,2cl Dry Curacao

2,2cl Lime juice

2 Dash bitter

Zubereitung: Shake. No ice double strain.

RED LION

2cl Gin

2cl Tripple Sec

1cl Lemon juice

2cl Orange juice

1cl Grenadine

Zubereitung: Shake. No ice double strain. Orange peel garnish.

SATAN´S WHISKERS

3cl Gin

1,5cl Dry Vermouth

1,5cl Roter Vermouth

1,5cl Tripple Sec

3cl Orange juice

1 Dash Orange bitter

Zubereitung: Shake. No ice double strain. Orange peel garnish.

SILVER FIZZ

6cl Gin

3cl Lemon juice

2,2cl Simple syrup

3/4 Egg White (Or whole if not L)

Zubereitung: Dry shake then ice shake. One ice cube. Fill Soda water to about half in Collins glass, then fill with the content of shaker.

Gin Fizz is without egg white.

SWEET MARTINI

3cl Gin

1,5cl White Vermouth

1,5cl Roter Vermouth

1,5cl Tripple Sec

3cl Orange juice

1 Dash Orange bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. No Ice double strain. Lime peel garnish.

THE VESPER

4,5cl Gin

1,5cl Vodka

0,75cl Lillet blanc

1 Dash bitter

Zubereitung: Shake. No Ice double strain. Lemon channel twist garnish.

A James Bond invented Cocktail!

TOM COLLINS

6cl Gin

3cl Lemon juice

1TL sugar

Zubereitung: Stir. Fill in Collins glass with big chunks of ice. Top with soda and lemon wedge garnish.

A James Bond favorite!

WHITE LADY

4,5cl Gin

2,2cl Tripple Sec

2,2cl Lemon juice

3/4 Egg White (Or whole if not L)

Zubereitung: Dry shake then ice shake. No ice.
