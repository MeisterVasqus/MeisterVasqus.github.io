FLYING CANGAROO

2cl Wodka

2cl White Rum

1cl Galliano

1cl Sahne

2cl Cream of Coconut

6cl Ananassaft

3cl Orangensaft

Zubereitung: Shake. Fill shaker content in OF glass with ice.

GOLDEN DREAM

1cl Galliano

1cl Sahne

1cl Tripple Sec

4cl Orangensaft

Zubereitung: Shake. No ice double strain. Orange peel garnish.

LONG ISLAND ICED TEA

1,5cl Wodka

1,5cl Gin

1,5cl White Rum

1,5cl Tequila Silver

1,5cl Tripple Sec

2,5cl Lemon juice

1cl Double Syrup

Zubereitung: Shake. Fill Collins glass with crushed ice. Top with cola and lemon wedge garnish.

TWELVE MILE LIMIT

3cl White Rum

1,5cl Rye

1,5cl Brandy

1,5cl Grenadine

1,5cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Shake. No ice double strain. Lemon peel garnish.
