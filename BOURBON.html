DNCocktails

Drinks mit Bourbon. Burbon ist mindestens 51% Mais und für 3 jahre, in frischen Eichenfässern gelagert.

BOURBON

BOULEVARDIER

3cl Bourbon/Rye

3cl Campari

3cl Red Vermouth

Zubereitung: Stir. Put in OF glass with big chunk of ice. Maraschino cherry tooth pick garnish.

BOURBON AND BRANCH

6cl Bourbon

0,9cl Simple syrup

2 Dash Bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. Put in OF glass with big chunk of ice. Orange peel garnish.

A James Bond favorite!

DERBY

4,5cl Bourbon (Use a higher Rye one, like Old Grand-dad, Bullet or Wild Turkey 101)

2,2cl Red Vermouth

1,5cl Dry Curacao

1,5cl Lime juice

Zubereitung: Shake. No ice and double strain. Mint garnish.

Trader Vic´s recipe!

GOLD RUSH

6cl Bourbon

3cl Honey syrup

2,2cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Shake. Double strain, fill in OF glass with big chunk of ice.

JACK TORRANCE

3cl Bourbon(Or Jack Daniels)

9cl Advocaat

2 Dash bitters

Zubereitung: Shake. Fill shaker content in OF glass.

LYNCHBURG LEMONADE

4cl Bourbon(Eigentlich Jack Daniel´s)

1cl Tripple Sec

2cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Shake. Fill in Collins glass or LL glass with big chunks of ice. Top with Lemonade and lemon wedge garnish.

MINT JULEP

6cl Bourbon (Best to use Wild Turkey 101)

3-4 Sprig mint

1,5cl Simple syrup

Zubereitung: Gently muddle mint in metal cup or big glass. Then fill cup with crushed ice, then add Whisky and garnish with mint and metal straw.

OLD FASHIONED

6cl Bourbon/Rye

6cl Branch water

Zubereitung: Put 1 big ice cube in OF glass, add all liquid and giv one little stir.

A James Bond favorite!

REVOLVER

6cl Bourbon (Use a higher Rye one, like Old Grand-dad or Bullet)

1,5cl Coffee liqor

1 Dash orange bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. FLamed orange peel garnish.

WHISKEY SOUR

4,5cl Bourbon (Or Whisky you like)

2,2cl Lemon juice

1,5cl Simple syrup

Half to 3/4 of egg white

Zubereitung: Dry shake than ice shake. No ice, top with 4-5 drops of bitters.
