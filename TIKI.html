Tiki drinks sind fruchtige jedoch starke drinks die oft auch hübsch geschmückt sind.

TIKI

ANCIENT MARINER

3cl Jamaican Rum

3cl Demerara Rum

1,5cl Simple syrup

1,5cl Lemon juice

1,5cl White grapefruite juice

2cl Lime juice

1TL Pimento dram

Zubereitung: Shake with 12oz crushed ice + 3 ice cubes. Fill shaker content in OF glass or TIKI mug. Carved lime wedge and mint garnish.

DOCTOR FUNK

7,5cl Jamaican Rum

0,75cl Absinth

1,5cl Lime juice

1,5cl Lemon juice

0,75cl Grenadine

1,5cl Rich simple syrup

Zubereitung: Shake with 12oz crushed ice + 3 ice cubes. Fill shaker content in OF glass. Lime shell with mint garnish.

FOG CUTTER

6cl White Rum

3cl Pisco/Brandy

1,5cl Gin

1,5cl Oregeat

3,5cl Lemon juice

4,5cl Orange juice

Zubereitung: Shake with 12oz crushed ice + 3 ice cubes. Fill shaker content in Pilsner glass. Lime shell with mint garnish.

JUNGLE BIRD

4,5cl Jamaican Rum

2,2cl Campari

1,5cl Simple syrup

1,5cl Lime juice

6cl Pineapple juice

Zubereitung: Shake with 12oz crushed ice + 3 ice cubes. Fill shaker content in OF glass or TIKI mug.Lemon M-Cherry toothpick, mint and orchid garnish.

MAI TAI

3cl Jamaican Rum

3cl Gold Rum agricole

2,2cl Lime juice

1,5cl Curacao dry

0,75cl Rich simple syrup

0,75cl Oregeat

Zubereitung: Shake with 12oz crushed ice + 3 ice cubes. Fill shaker content in OF glass. Lime shell with mint garnish.

MISSIONARYS DOWNFALL

3cl White Rum

1,5cl Peachtree

1,5cl Lime juice

3cl Honey syrup

2oz Tightly packed mint leafs.

2oz Pineapple chunks about sugar cube size.

Zubereitung: Blend all with 6oz of crushed ice. Fill with no ice in coupe glass and garnish with mint.

MR. BALI HAI

4,5cl Jamaican Rum

3cl White Rum

2,2cl Coffee liqor

1,5cl Simple syrup

3cl Lemon juice

4,5cl Pineapple juice

Zubereitung: Shake with 12oz crushed ice + 3 ice cubes. Fill shaker content in OF glass or TIKI mug.

PLANTERS PUNCH

9cl Gold Rum

2,5cl Lime juice

1cl Lemon juice

1,5cl Grenadine

1/4TL Sugar

Zubereitung: Stir. Fill glass with crushed. Fill Stir glass content in collins glass and top with soda, if not full to the top.

SUFFERING BASTARD

3cl Brandy

3cl Gin

1,5cl Lime Cordial

2 Dash Bitters

Zubereitung: Shake. Fill in Collins glass or TIKI mug with big ice cubes. Top with Gingerbeer. Orange and mint garnish.

ZOMBI

3cl Overproof Rum (>60% Demerara Rum)

4,5cl Jamaican Rum

4,5cl Gold Cuban Aged Rum

2,2cl Lime juice

1,5cl Falernum

1,5cl Dons Mix (White grapefruite juice and cinnamon sirup)

1TL Grenadine

1 Dash Angostura bitters

1 Dash (6drops) Absinth

Zubereitung: Shake with 6oz crushed ice. Fill shaker content in Collins glass or TIKI mug with mint garnish.
