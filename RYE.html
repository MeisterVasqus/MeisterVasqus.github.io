DNCocktails

Whisky mit mindestens 51% Rye anteil (Roggen).

RYE

THE BLINKER

6cl RYE

3cl White grapefruite juice

1TL Grenadine

Zubereitung: Shake. Double strain. No ice. Lemon peel garnish.

COCKTAIL A LA LOUISIANE

2,2cl RYE

2,2cl Dom Benediktin

2,2cl Red Vermouth

3 Dash Absinth

3 Dash Peychauds Bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Maraschino Cherry garnish.

THE CRANSTON

6cl RYE

1,5cl Himbeersirup

2 Dash Bitter

Zubereitung: Fill Julep Cup or OF glass with crushed ice. Press the Basil leafs in Mixing glass. Stir. Strain in cup. Basil garnish.

JAPALAC

2,2cl RYE

2,2cl Dry Vermouth

1cl Orange juice

1TL Himbeersirup

Zubereitung: Shake. Double strain. No ice. Orange peel garnish.

MANHATTAN

6cl RYE

3cl Red Vermouth

2 Dash Bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Maraschino Cherry.

MONTE CARLO

6cl RYE

1,5cl Dom Benediktin

2 Dash Bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Lemon peel garnish.

REMEMBER THE MAINE

4,5cl RYE

2TL Kirschlikör

2,2cl Red Vermouth

0,5TL Absinth

Zubereitung: Stir. No Ice. Lemon peel garnish.

RYE BUCK

6cl RYE

0,75cl Lime juice

Splash ginger sirup

Zubereitung: Buuild in Collins glass with big chunk of ice. Top with gingerbeer.

SAZERAC

9cl RYE

1 Sugar cube

3 Dash Peychauds Bitter

Absinth glass cover

Zubereitung: Sugar Cube in mixing glass hit with bitters muttle. Stir. No ice. Lemon peel garnish.

SCOFFLAW

4,5cl RYE

3cl Dry Vermouth

2,2cl Grenadine

2,2cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Shake. No ice. Lemon peel garnish.

VIEX CARRE

3cl RYE

3cl Brandy

1TL Dom Benediktin

3cl Red Vermouth

2 Dash Bitter

2 Dash Peychauds Bitter

Zubereitung: Stir. Double strain, fill in OF glass with big chunk of ice. Lemon peel garnish.

WHISKEY RICKEY

6cl RYE

2,2cl Lime juice

Zubereitung: Build in glass. Collins glass with big chunk of ice. Top with soda.

ZIONCHECK ZIPPER

4,5cl RYE

1,5cl Honey Sirup (3:1)

6 Minzblätter

3cl Red Vermouth

2 Dash Bitter

2 Dash Peychauds Bitter

Zubereitung: Die Minzblätter leicht andrücken mit dem Honig. Stir with crushed ice. Fill in OF glass with crushed ice. Mint garnish.
