FAVORITES

Unsere lieblings Cocktails.

BOURBON

GOLD RUSH

6cl Bourbon

3cl Honey syrup

2,2cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Shake. Double strain, fill in OF glass with big chunk of ice.

LYNCHBURG LEMONADE

4cl Bourbon(Eigentlich Jack Daniel´s)

1cl Tripple Sec

2cl Lemon juice

Zubereitung: Shake. Fill in Collins glass or LL glass with big chunks of ice. Top with Lemonade and lemon wedge garnish.

OLD FASHIONED

6cl Bourbon/Rye

6cl Branch water

Zubereitung: Put 1 big ice cube in OF glass, add all liquid and giv one little stir.

A James Bond favorite!

WHISKEY SOUR

4,5cl Bourbon (Or Whisky you like)

2,2cl Lemon juice

1,5cl Simple syrup

Half to 3/4 of egg white

Zubereitung: Dry shake than ice shake. No ice, top with 4-5 drops of bitters.

OTHERS

GOLDEN DREAM

1cl Galliano

1cl Sahne

1cl Tripple Sec

4cl Orangensaft

Zubereitung: Shake. No ice double strain. Orange peel garnish.

Natasch:"Schmeckt wie Ed v. Schleck Eis!"

LONG ISLAND ICED TEA

1,5cl Wodka

1,5cl Gin

1,5cl White Rum

1,5cl Tequila Silver

1,5cl Tripple Sec

2,5cl Lemon juice

1cl Double Syrup

Zubereitung: Shake. Fill Collins glass with crushed ice. Top with cola and lemon wedge garnish.

TIKI

RUM RUNNER

1,5cl Gold Rum

1,5cl White Rum

1,5cl Orange juice

1,5cl Pineapple juice

1,5cl Brombeerlikör

1,5cl Bannanenlikör

1TL Grenadine

#1 1 Dash bitter

Zubereitung: Shake. Fill shaker content in OF glass.

WODKA

MOSCOW MULE

#1 5cl Wodka(#2 6cl)

#1 1cl Honey syrup(3:1)

#1 3cl Lime juice(#2 2cl)

#1 #2 1,5cl Ginger syrup

#1 1 Dash bitter

Zubereitung: Shake. Fill shaker content in OF glass or MM mug. Top with Gingerbeer. Mint Lime garnish, or gingercandy.

VERY BLUEBERRY

4cl Wodka

2cl Brombeerlikör

2cl Lime juice

1cl Simple sirup

2EL Blaubeeren

Zubereitung: Andrücken Shake. Fill in OF glass with ice.
