DNCocktails

Die vielseitigste Sorte Alkohol.

Rum

BACARDI

4,5cl Bacardi(White Rum)

3cl Lime juice

0,75cl Grenadine

Zubereitung: Shake. Double strain. No ice.

CUBA LIBRE

6cl Cuban Rum

12cl Cola

1 Lime

Zubereitung: Muttle quarterd lime in Shaker. Than add Rum in collins glass with ice. Top with coke.

CUBAN OLD FASHIONED

6cl Cuban Rum

0,75cl Simple Sirup

1 Dash Tripple Sec

2 Dash Bitter

2 Dash Orange Bitter

1/4TL graded Lime peel

Zubereitung:Stir. Double strain OF glass with big ice. Mint leaf garnish.

Daiquiri

6cl White Rum

1cl Simple Sirup

1,5cl Lime juice

Zubereitung: Shake. No Ice. Lime wheel garnish.

DARK´N STORMY

6cl Goslings Black Rum (Dark Rum)

Zubereitung: Build in collins glass. First Ice then gingerbeer. Fill in Rum and lightly stir. Lime wheel garnish.

DIRTY SHIRLEY

6cl Gold Rum

1TL Grenadine

Splash ginger sirup

Zubereitung: Build in Collins glass with big chunk of ice. Top with gingerale. Maraschino cherry garnish.

MOJITO

6cl White Rum

2,2cl Lime juice

3cl Simple Sirup

12 Minzblätter

Zubereitung: Mintblätter mit Sirup leicht andrücken. Shake. Big chunk of ice in collins glass. Top with soda and mint garnish.

Presidente

4,5cl White Rum

1TL Tripple Sec

4,5cl White Vermouth

1/4TL Grenadine

Zubereitung: Stir. No ice. Maraschino Cherry and Orange peel garnish

Redrum

4,5cl Jamaican Rum

1,5cl Campari

1,5cl Cherry Liqor

0,75cl Tripple Sec

1/4TL graded Lime zest

Zubereitung: Stir. Double strain. No ice.
